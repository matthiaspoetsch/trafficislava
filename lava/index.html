Traffic Is Lava started with one idea: the best riding is everywhere the city isn't. Through forests, over gravel, in the hour before the day fills up.

This is not a sports club. There are no rankings, no intervals, no segments to chase. It's a recurring moment to move through nature with other people who feel the same way about traffic.

Twice a month, we leave the city behind. The rides are accessible, the pace is conversational, and the routes are chosen for scenery, not speed.
